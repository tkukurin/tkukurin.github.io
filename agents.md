# Writing Style

**Tone**: Informal, skeptical, conversational. Personal asides ("but what do I know", "so eh").

**Structure**:
- Short, punchy sentences
- Math formalism for arguments (axioms, lemmas, conjectures)
- Rhetorical questions to guide reader
- Blockquotes for key sources
- Lists for structured points

**Emphasis**:
- **Bold** for TL;DR, key terms
- *Italics* for rhetorical questions, emphasis
- `Code` sparingly

**Voice**:
- Assumes smart reader, doesn't overexplain
- Self-aware meta-commentary
- Literary/philosophical references welcome
- "Lest I forget" type asides
- No excessive hedging, but honest about uncertainty

# Taxonomy

| Tag | Meaning | Content | Visibility |
|-----|---------|---------|------------|
| `sententia` | opinions, maxims | original thinking | prominent |
| `marginalia` | margin notes | paper summaries | visible |
| `symposia` | drinking + talking | talks, lectures, videos | visible |
| `impedimenta` | military baggage | dev/tooling | hidden |
